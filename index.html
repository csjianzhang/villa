Openings

Postdoctoral Fellows

The VILLA group at PKU is looking for a top postdoctoral researcher with expertise in computer vision, computational photography, and deep learning. The researcher for this opening will work at PKU to investigate and develop principled computational methods for highly effective, scalable, and robust visual-information intelligent learning (e.g. person re-identification, tracking, segmentation, compression, reconstruction). Please send a full CV with a complete list of your publications and projects to Prof. Jian Zhang.

Students

The VILLA group at PKU is looking for motivated MS and Ph.D. students in computer vision and signal processing. Please send a full CV with a complete list of your publications and projects, as well as, an unofficial academic transcript to Prof. Jian Zhang.
